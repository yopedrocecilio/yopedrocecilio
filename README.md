#### things i believe

- talent is everywhere. access is not. that's a design problem, not a law of nature.
- in a post-AGI world, the scarce thing isn't skill ‚ÄĒ it's taste, judgment, and knowing what to build.
- community > platform. a network of people with shared values compounds in ways software can't.
- proof of work > proof of credentials. always.
- the best people i've ever met were in places the market wasn't looking.

#### things i think about

- what happens to human identity when anyone can build anything
- why the most talented builders are still invisible and what infrastructure fixes that
- how to build a company that feels like a movement, not a transaction
- the gap between where talent lives and where attention clusters
- what "work" even means when AI does the execution and humans do the taste

#### on people

i've talked to thousands of engineers across latin america over the past few years. the pattern is clear: the best ones don't have the best resumes. they have the best side projects, the most opinionated takes on architecture, and the kind of hunger you can't fake. the world is sleeping on these people. not for long.
