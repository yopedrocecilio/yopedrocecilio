### what i'm building

**[BeGlobal](https://beglobal.work)** — a community of 3,000+ of the world's best builders. mostly from Latin America. engineers who've shipped real products at real startups, who care about craft, and who prove themselves through work — not credentials.

we started by placing elite talent into early-stage teams (48h, pre-vetted, bootstrapped, profitable). but the long game was always the community. a living network of builders with shared values: ownership, speed, taste, honesty. everything we build next compounds on top of that.

**[UNDV](https://undv.com)** — the next layer. a platform where AI builders get identity, discovery, connection, and monetization. if you're shipping with lovable, replit, v0, claude, cursor — we're building this for you. pre-launch.

<br>

### things i believe

> talent is everywhere. access is not. that's a design problem.

- in a post-AGI world, the scarce thing isn't skill — it's taste, judgment, and knowing *what* to build
- community > platform. shared values compound in ways software alone can't
- the best people i've met were in places the market wasn't looking
- proof of work > proof of credentials. always
- when anyone can build anything, identity shifts from *what you do* to *who you are* and *what you've shipped*

<br>

### on builders

i've spent years talking to thousands of engineers across latin america. the pattern is clear: the best ones don't have the best resumes. they have the best side projects, the sharpest opinions on architecture, and a kind of hunger you can't fake.

the world is sleeping on these people. not for long.
